SeaBubbbles Dive Tours

SeaBubbles Hawaii is a scuba diving company located in Waikiki, Hawaii. We offer a variety of scuba diving packages for divers of all levels, from beginners to advanced divers.

Our team of experienced scuba instructors and guides are passionate about exploring the underwater world and sharing their knowledge with others. We pride ourselves on providing a safe and enjoyable scuba diving experience for all our customers.

What to Expect

  • Scuba Tours last roughly 3 hours
  • Equipment and snacks included
  • Convenient location with free shuttling available

Dive Times

  • 8 A.M. Dive for certified divers
  • 1 P.M. Dive for beginner divers
